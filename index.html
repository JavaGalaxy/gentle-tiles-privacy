Privacy Policy for Gentle Tiles

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Gentle Tiles ("the App") is a memory-matching tile game. This page explains what information the App does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

Gentle Tiles does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers. The App does not require an account, login, or internet connection to play.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App saves a small amount of data locally on your device using standard on-device storage, including:

  • Your preferences (such as sound/vibration and text size settings)
  • Your game statistics (best score, games won, current level)
  • Your in-progress game, so you can resume where you left off

This data never leaves your device, is not shared with us or any third party, and is removed automatically if you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
